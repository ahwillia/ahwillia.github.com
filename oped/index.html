Things I Feel Strongly About

As an ex-high school debater, I cannot pass up the opportunity to throw my personal opinions up on the web. I believe that scientists should engage in social/political/cultural discourse to a greater extent, so here are my two cents.

People need to understand what scientific innovation can bring us in the near future

Neil deGrasse Tyson is fond of saying that Americans have "stopped dreaming." I strongly support space exploration and restoring proper levels of funding to NASA, but I think it is more important that scientists extend this message to encompass other areas of research. In short, biology and neuroscience need a Neil deGrasse Tyson.

I would love it if Ray Kurzweil, and Aubrey de Grey were to become household names. (Even though they are a bit crazy.)
