About

I'm a postdoc in Statistics at Stanford University working with Scott Linderman's research group on a variety of data analytic challenges in neuroscience and biology.

I completed my PhD while working in Surya Ganguli's group at Stanford, and was funded through the DOE Computational Science Graduate Fellowship Program. Before that, I worked at the Salk Institute (with Terry Sejnowski) and Brandeis University (with Eve Marder and Tim O'Leary). I performed my undergraduate studies at Bowdoin College, where I worked with Patsy Dickinson.
